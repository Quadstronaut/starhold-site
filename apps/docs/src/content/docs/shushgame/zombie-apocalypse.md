On the **15th of every month** at midnight UTC, the graveyard cracks open.
For seven days (days 15–21), the dead may rise as **Zombies** — allowed back
into **#shushchat** under the inverted law:

> Every word a zombie types must be forbidden — a word already on the
> kill-list. The dead speak only in forbidden tongues.

## Rising

React 🧟 to the gate message pinned in **#zombie-briefing** during an active
Apocalypse. That's the only way back into the arena that week. Decline and
you sit out the apocalypse — but the grave still opens at round's end
regardless.

## The kill bounty

Zombies hunt the living by baiting them into echoing banned words. Every time
a living player falls during the apocalypse, the last zombies who spoke in
**#shushchat** before they died earn **40 points each** — up to 5 zombies,
within the last 10 minutes, no splitting. Crowd the arena; the more you
speak, the more you cash in when someone slips.

The kill-list only grows, so late-week zombies feast: early in the month the
horde rises lean, but by the final days the banned list is fat and the living
are trapped.

## Total victory

If 90% of active players end up dead, every zombie is fully restored — and
their victims stay buried.

## ⚖️ Purgatory

One clean word — a single living syllable — and a zombie is damned. No
warning, no second chance. **Purgatory** claims you immediately: you can see
and speak only in **#purgatory**, and nothing gets you out before round's
end. Buyback, gauntlet, and community vote are all refused. The round-end
ceremony frees everyone.

## The endgame

When the apocalypse closes on day 22, the remaining living play out the rest
of the month under the heaviest ban pressure of the round. Survive to the
final whistle and a permanent champion role is yours.
