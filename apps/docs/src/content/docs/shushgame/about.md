**The Quiet Game.** A Discord server that is itself the game: every day, one
word becomes forbidden. Say it — or anything *containing* it — and you die.
Silence is golden.

Lives at [shushgame.com](https://shushgame.com). Free to play. The dying is
always free.

## The loop

1. You talk in **#shushchat**. Every word is counted.
2. At **midnight UTC**, the machine studies yesterday's ten most-used words
   and bans one at random. The decree lands in **#daily-decree**; the full
   list of banned words lives in **#kill-list**.
3. The banned list only grows. Every day of the round is deadlier than the
   last.
4. Type a banned word — even buried inside another word — and you die.
   Silently. Immediately.

## The round calendar

A round is one calendar month (all times UTC):

| Days | Phase |
|---|---|
| 1–3 | **Grace period** — words are counted, nobody dies |
| 4–14 | The Quiet Game proper — daily decrees, daily deaths |
| 15–21 | **🧟 Zombie Apocalypse** — the dead rise ([details](/shushgame/zombie-apocalypse/)) |
| 22–end | **Endgame** — the apocalypse closes; the living play out the month under a long, fat kill-list |

At round's end: awards, every mute lifted, the kill-list resets, and it all
begins again.

## The website

[shushgame.com](https://shushgame.com) shows the live decree board, the kill
list, the countdown to the next decree — and turns zombie-green automatically
while an Apocalypse is active.
